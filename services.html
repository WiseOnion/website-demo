Services

We offer a range of behavioral health services — individual, couples, family, groups, psychiatric evaluation, and supervised intern services. Evening and weekend appointments are available.

Core Clinical Services

Individual Therapy

Evidence-based approaches including CBT, ACT, and trauma-informed care tailored to each client.

Couples & Relationship Therapy

Communication-focused work that helps partners rebuild trust, improve connection, and solve ongoing conflicts.

Family Therapy

Systems-based approaches to strengthen family communication and address presenting concerns with all members involved.

Specialty & Supportive Services

Child & Adolescent Therapy

Play-based, family-inclusive therapy for children and teens.

Group Therapy & Workshops

Structured groups for skills-building (anxiety, DBT-skills, parenting), and periodic workshops.

Psychiatric Evaluation

Medication evaluation and coordination with prescribing providers when clinically appropriate.

Intern Services (Supervised)

Our clinic hosts practicum and internship students from accredited programs. These clinicians see clients as part of their supervised training. If you choose intern services, you receive quality care at a reduced rate while helping train the next generation of clinicians.

How Intern Services Work

  • Supervision: All interns are supervised by licensed clinicians who review cases, co-sign notes, and provide oversight.
  • Cost: Intern sessions are typically offered at a reduced fee compared with fully licensed providers.
  • Matching: After intake, we match clients to interns based on clinical needs, availability, and language/cultural preferences.
  • Transition: If a client requires ongoing, specialized, or medication management care, we discuss options to transition to a licensed clinician.
  • Confidentiality: Interns follow the same privacy and HIPAA rules as staff; supervision is part of the clinical process and is protected.

Intake & Treatment Process

Our intake process is designed to be welcoming and efficient. Typical steps:

  • Initial contact & scheduling of an intake appointment.
  • Comprehensive intake assessment (clinical history, strengths, and goals).
  • Treatment planning with measurable goals and a recommended cadence.
  • Ongoing review of progress, outcomes tracking, and coordination with other providers when needed.

FAQs

Do you accept insurance?
Yes — we accept many major insurers and offer a sliding scale for uninsured clients. Insurance specifics are confirmed during intake.
Are sessions available virtually?
Yes — we offer telehealth sessions for many service types depending on client needs and provider licensing.
How do you assign interns?
After intake we consider client goals, language, availability, and intern training focus to find the best match. Supervisors remain engaged in the care plan.
What if I need medication?
We can provide psychiatric evaluations and coordinate with prescribing providers. If long-term medication management is needed, we will discuss appropriate referrals.

Outcomes & Measurement

We use standardized measures and client-reported outcomes to track progress and adjust treatment plans. Your clinician will discuss how progress is measured and celebrated.
